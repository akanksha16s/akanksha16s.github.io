Why government of India should subsidise Momos?

Proposal for Food Policy Reform
  • 1. Nutritional Value: Boiled vegetables, soybeans, paneer & chicken.
  • 2. Diversification: Good for the taste pallet.
  • 3. Employment: Especially for the people from North-Eastern states of India.
  • 4. Unity: Strengthens cultural sensitivity; unites people on the lines of food preferences.
  • 5. Soft Power: Enhances bonds with countries like Nepal.
Way Forward
  • • Promote "Momos par Manthan" on the lines of "Chai par Charcha".
  • • Inclusion of Maida in PDS.
  • • R&D to replace Maida with other nutritious yet tasty flour.
