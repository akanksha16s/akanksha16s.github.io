The Virtue of Jealousy

A Manifesto by the All India Chugli Association (AICA)

From time immemorial, we have been taught that jealousy is a negative feeling, born out of insecurity. I strongly disagree with this view. I believe jealousy is a vital and sophisticated emotion that humans have developed through evolution.

As President of the All India Chugli Association (AICA), I present the following reasons why jealousy should be recognised as a positive force:

1. It serves as an internal compass, clearly showing us what we truly desire and what is currently missing from our lives.
2. It promotes humility by reminding us that we are only human, not divine beings.
3. It provides powerful motivation, encouraging us to stay disciplined and work consistently toward our goals.
4. When expressed honestly to the person we are jealous of, it becomes one of the highest compliments possible—an act of transparency that can deepen relationships.
5. It sharpens the mind and enhances one's sense of humour, as the author of this manifesto can personally attest.

Therefore, I believe the most effective way to engage with jealousy is through complete acceptance. Acceptance is the key.

As President of AICA, we fully endorse the experience of jealousy. No one should feel ashamed of this powerful evolutionary gift.

"May the flame of jealousy continue to burn within you!"
Akanksha Singh
President, AICA
Chandrataal - B8
