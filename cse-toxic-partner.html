CSE and a Toxic Partner

By: An extremely doubtful aspirant

  • 1. You love both of them.
  • 2. You cannot even imagine your life without both of them.
  • 3. They make you dream about a beautiful future, with suffering in the present.
  • 4. They 'love-bomb' you initially, and slowly become 'emotionally draining'.
  • 5. They might force you to become disconnected from your immediate surrounding.
  • 6. They always make you doubt your personality and choices ("It's not them, it's you").
  • 7. They make you feel guilty about having some "me time".
  • 8. They take credit even for your smallest success.
  • 9. A toxic partner makes you feel guilty for interacting with someone else; CSE preparation makes you feel guilty about reading books outside the course.
  • 10. They constantly remind you about your vulnerabilities and past mistakes.
  • 11. No matter how much you make an effort, nothing is enough for them.
  • 12. You change yourself and make a lot of compromises just for them.
  • 13. Even though you love them, you always have doubts about actually spending the rest of your life with them.
  • 14. In the end, there is a high chance they will choose someone else over you with random reasons (e.g., Bhosale Sir marks hi nahi dete, Abba nahi manenge).
